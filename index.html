The Problem

Cold email is broken.
Here's why yours isn't working.

Most outreach fails before the prospect even reads it. These are the four reasons — and why AdoBoost solves all of them.

📤

You're sending in bulk

Blasting 200 emails at once is a red flag for every major email provider. Domain reputation tanks. Future emails — even to warm leads — land in junk.

🔇

Follow-ups never happen

80% of replies come from follow-up #2 or #3. But manual follow-ups get skipped because you're busy. Leads go cold while you're working other deals.

✍️

Writing cold emails is hard

Staring at a blank email for 45 minutes only to get no reply is demoralising. Most people copy generic templates — and prospects know it immediately.

🥶

Your inbox is cold

New domain? Fresh email account? Sending from a cold inbox is like calling from an unknown number. Warming it manually takes weeks and is easy to get wrong.
